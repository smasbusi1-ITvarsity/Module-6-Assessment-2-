Candyman is a 2021 supernatural slasher film directed by Nia DasCosta and written

by Jordan Peele, Win Rosenfeld and DaCosta. The film is a direct sequel to the

1992 film of the same name and the fourth film in the Candyman film series

based on the short story "The forbidden" by Clive Barker. The film stars

Yahya Adbul-Mateen II, Teyonah Parris, Nathan Stewart-Jarrett, Colman Domingo

and Kyle Kaminsky. Venessa Williams, Virginia Madsen and Tony Tod aslo reprise

their roles from the original film.
